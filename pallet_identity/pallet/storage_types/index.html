Structs§

  • AccountOfUsername
    Reverse lookup from username to the AccountId that has registered it. The value should be a key in the IdentityOf map, but it may not if the user has cleared their identity.
  • IdentityOf
    Information that is pertinent to identify the entity behind an account. First item is the registration, second is the account’s primary username.
  • PendingUsernames
    Usernames that an authority has granted, but that the account controller has not confirmed that they want it. Used primarily in cases where the AccountId cannot provide a signature because they are a pure proxy, multisig, etc. In order to confirm it, they should call Call::accept_username.
  • Registrars
    The set of registrars. Not expected to get very big as can only be added through a special origin (likely a council motion).
  • SubsOf
    Alternative “sub” identities of this account.
  • SuperOf
    The super-identity of an alternative “sub” identity together with its name, within that context. If the account is not some other account’s sub-identity, then just None.
  • UsernameAuthorities
    A map of the accounts who are authorized to grant usernames.
