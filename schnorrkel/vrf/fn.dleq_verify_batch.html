Batch verify DLEQ proofs where the public keys were held by different parties.

We first reconstruct the cs present in the VRFProofs but absent in the VRFProofBatchables, using shorten_dleq. We then verify the R and Hr components of the VRFProofBatchables using the two equations a normal verification uses to discover them. We do this by delinearizing both verification equations with random numbers.
